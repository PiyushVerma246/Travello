    Package Overview

    Escape to paradise with our exclusive Maldives package! Experience the ultimate tropical getaway with crystal-clear waters, pristine white beaches, and world-class luxury resorts. This 7-day journey includes everything you need for an unforgettable vacation - from direct flights and 5-star accommodation to exciting water activities and gourmet dining.

    Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of the Maldives, where every moment is a postcard-perfect memory. Whether you're seeking romance, adventure, or pure relaxation, this package offers the perfect blend of all three.

    Day-by-Day Itinerary

    1

    Arrival & Resort Check-in

    Arrive at Malé International Airport where our representative will greet you. Transfer to your luxury resort via speedboat or seaplane. Check-in to your overwater villa and enjoy a welcome drink. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the resort facilities. Evening welcome dinner at the resort's beachfront restaurant.

    2

    Snorkeling & Island Exploration

    After breakfast, embark on a guided snorkeling tour to explore vibrant coral reefs and marine life. Visit a local island to experience Maldivian culture and shop for souvenirs. Enjoy a traditional Maldivian lunch. Return to resort for relaxation. Optional sunset cruise available in the evening.

    3

    Water Sports & Spa Day

    Morning water sports activities including jet skiing, kayaking, and paddleboarding. Afternoon spa session with traditional Maldivian massage treatments. Evening at leisure to enjoy resort amenities. Romantic dinner under the stars can be arranged.

    4

    Dolphin Watching & Sandbank Picnic

    Early morning dolphin watching cruise. Witness playful dolphins in their natural habitat. Visit a pristine sandbank for a private picnic lunch. Afternoon free for swimming and sunbathing. Return to resort for dinner and evening entertainment.

    5

    Scuba Diving Adventure

    Full day scuba diving experience (for certified divers) or introductory dive session. Explore underwater caves, shipwrecks, and encounter manta rays and sea turtles. Lunch on a nearby island. Evening BBQ dinner on the beach with live music.

    6

    Leisure Day & Sunset Fishing

    Day at leisure to enjoy resort facilities - infinity pool, private beach, or in-villa relaxation. Optional activities available. Evening traditional Maldivian fishing trip. Your catch can be prepared for dinner by the resort chef. Farewell dinner with special cultural performance.

    7

    Departure

    Enjoy a leisurely breakfast. Check-out from resort and transfer to Malé International Airport for your departure flight. Take home unforgettable memories of your Maldives paradise experience.
